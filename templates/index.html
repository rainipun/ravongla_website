Discover Himalayan Hospitality Where People Make the Place

Himalayan hospitality embodies the unique blend of warmth, cultural richness, and natural beauty of the Himalayan region. It features personalized, friendly service rooted in local traditions and sustainable practices. Visitors experience cultural immersion through local cuisine, festivals, and homestays. Eco-friendly accommodations and community-based tourism ensure environmental and local benefits. The serene landscapes, biodiversity, and historical sites add to the allure, offering a holistic and memorable experience that reflects the genuine warmth and heritage of the Himalayan people. Himalayan hospitality is renowned for its genuine warmth and friendliness. Hosts often go out of their way to make guests feel at home. Services are tailored to meet the individual needs and preferences of guests, providing a more personalized experience. Himalayan Hospitality offers a blend of cultural richness, personalized service, sustainable practices, adventure, wellness, and natural beauty, creating a unique and memorable experience for visitors.

Discover Unique Experience at Three Distinct Locations

Ravongla Star

Nestled in the tranquil town of Ravongla, Sikkim, Ravongla Star offers stunning mountain views and unparalleled hospitality, providing a serene retreat for relaxation and rejuvenation. Experience the perfect blend of comfort and natural beauty in the heart of the Himalayas.

Bamboo Inn

Situated in the vibrant city of Gangtok, Sikkim, Bamboo Inn features exquisite bamboo interiors that create a warm and inviting ambiance. Enjoy modern comforts and unique design elements that reflect the natural beauty of the region.

Valley View

Located in Pelling, Valley View offers breathtaking vistas of Mt. Kanchenjunga, providing guests with an unforgettable scenic experience. Revel in the comfort and tranquility of our well-appointed rooms, perfectly complemented by stunning mountain views.
